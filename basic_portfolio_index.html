About Me

As the descendant of Mexican fieldworkers in the US, Brian made an early decision in his career to ensure that individuals from socioeconomically disadvantaged communities have access to a quality education. With a decade of global leadership experience, Brian has worked across the world developing highly effective training and leadership development programs for young educators. With his newfound skill set in coding, Brian aspires to weave together his expertise and passion for education with the use of mobile technology.

Currently, Brian is a Managing Director with Teacher For America in Denver and is enrolled in a coding boot camp through the University of Denver. He possesses a Master’s in International Educational Development from Columbia University and received his teaching credentials from the University of Hawai'i at Mānoa. He graduated cum laude from Pepperdine University with a Bachelor’s degree in International Studies with double minor in Spanish and French. In his spare time, Brian is currently learning Portuguese and Bahasa Malaysia.

In the fall of 2019, Brian hopes to pursue a doctoral degree in Instructional Technology and Media. His research will focus on how mobile technology can be leveraged in order to improve the educational outcomes for students in developing contexts.
